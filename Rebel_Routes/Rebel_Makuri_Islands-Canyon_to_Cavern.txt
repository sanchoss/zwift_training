============================================================
  REBEL ROUTE: CANYON TO CAVERN
============================================================

World:       Makuri Islands
Distance:    13.41 km
Elevation:   87 m
Start Route: Turf N Surf (then U-Turn, left toward Urukazi)
Approach:    Turf N Surf → U-turn → left toward Urukazi → descend through
             cavern → right at bottom onto beachfront → Tidepool Sprint banner
Strava:      https://www.strava.com/segments/33397045
VeloViewer:  https://veloviewer.com/segment/33397045
ZwiftInsider: https://zwiftinsider.com/canyon-to-cavern/

Description: Shortest loop covering both the slot canyon (Yumezi-Urukazi) and
  the cavern (Neokyo-Urukazi) connector roads. Starts/ends at Tidepool Sprint
  banner in Urukazi. Clockwise: climb slot canyon, flat through Neokyo, descend
  through cavern back to beach.

----------------------------------------
  TURN-BY-TURN DIRECTIONS
----------------------------------------

  Start/Finish: Tidepool Sprint banner (Urukazi beachfront)
  Pro tip: just stay right at every turn!

  (km values are approximate)

    1. [  2.0 km]  Straight/Right to Yumezi (top of slot canyon climb)
    2. [  4.0 km]  Right to Neokyo (dirt road junction)
    3. [  5.5 km]  Straight/Right to Tower (entering Neokyo)
    4. [  6.5 km]  Straight/Right to Tower (in Neokyo)
    5. [  7.5 km]  Right to Harbor (edge of Neokyo)
    6. [  8.5 km]  Right to Harbor (heading toward cavern)
    7. [ 10.0 km]  Right to Urukazi (cavern descent)
    8. [ 12.5 km]  Right to Hidden Reefs (approaching Tidepool Sprint)
